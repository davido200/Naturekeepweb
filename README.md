# NatureKeep | Restore, Protect, Thrive

NatureKeep is a community-led conservation initiative dedicated to restoring degraded land, protecting biodiversity, and fighting climate change in Kenya. We believe in the power of local communities to lead environmental transformation through sustainable practices and direct action.

## 🌿 Mission & Vision

- **Mission:** To restore degraded land and protect biodiversity in Kenya through community-led tree planting, wildlife protection, and sustainable environmental practices.
- **Vision:** A greener, healthier Kenya where nature and communities thrive together.

## 🚀 Key Focus Areas

- **🌳 Reforestation:** Restoring degraded land through community-led tree planting campaigns to combat climate change.
- **💧 Water Conservation:** Protecting vital watersheds and wetlands to ensure clean, accessible water and resilient ecosystems.
- **🐘 Wildlife Protection:** Safeguarding natural habitats and minimizing human-wildlife conflict across diverse landscapes.
- **🌍 Environmental Education:** Equipping citizens and youth with the knowledge to take meaningful environmental action.

## 🛠️ Project Structure

The project is a static website built with a focus on clean design and accessibility.

```text

├── index.html          # Home page - Overview and impact snapshot
├── about.html          # Our story, values, and team
├── what-we-do.html     # Detailed focus areas and initiatives
├── projects.html       # Ongoing and completed project showcase
├── contact.html        # Get involved - Volunteer, Donate, Partner
├── css/

├── js/
│   └── main.js        # Interactive elements and animations
└── assets/             # Images and branding (logo, nature photos)
```

## 💻 Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3 (Vanilla CSS), JavaScript.
- **Fonts:** Google Fonts (Montserrat, Open Sans, Poppins).
- **Design:** Modern, mobile-responsive layout with a focus on nature-inspired aesthetics.
