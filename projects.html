Our Projects

Below are examples of the types of projects we undertake or plan to implement. Each project aims to deliver measurable environmental and social benefits.

Reforestation & Land Restoration

Goal: Plant native tree species to improve soil fertility, increase biodiversity, and sequester carbon.

Activities: Community planting days, nurseries, and growth monitoring.

Impact: Increased habitat for wildlife and improved water retention.
