Active Work

Current and flagship interventions

Reforestation and Land Restoration

Goal: Plant native tree species to improve soil fertility, increase biodiversity, and sequester carbon.

Activities: Community planting days, nursery support, and growth monitoring.

Impact: Stronger habitats, lower erosion, and better long-term land recovery.

Support This Work

Water and Wetland Conservation

Goal: Protect key watersheds and improve water quality for ecosystems and communities.

Activities: Riparian planting, clean-up drives, and awareness campaigns.

Impact: Better water access, reduced runoff, and healthier riparian zones.

Partner on Water

Community Nursery Development

Goal: Build local capacity to propagate resilient indigenous species at scale.

Activities: Nursery training, seed handling, and stewardship coordination.

Impact: A steady pipeline of healthy seedlings and stronger local ownership.
