About Us

Who We Are

Community-led conservation with long-term stewardship in mind

NatureKeep is a conservation organization focused on protecting and restoring Kenya's natural ecosystems for current and future generations. Our work combines field action, education, and partnership with the communities most connected to the land.

We believe lasting conservation depends on local leadership, biodiversity protection, and practical systems that keep restored landscapes healthy over time.

01

Our Mission

To conserve, restore, and sustainably manage natural resources through community-led action, education, and partnership.

02

Why We Exist

Healthy ecosystems provide clean water, fertile soils, and habitats for diverse species. Without conservation, both nature and livelihoods are at risk.

03

How We Work

We blend restoration, education, advocacy, and community engagement to deliver measurable environmental outcomes.
